Book Club and Social Emotional Intelligence

Learning Goal

  1. To inculcate a habit of book reading among the students, for improving their vocabulary, comprehension, critical thinking.
  2. To make the students self-aware and manage their emotions, develop social awareness and social skills.

Syllabus

1. The Conch Bearer - Chitra Banerjee Divakaruni
2. The Alchemist - Paulo Coelho

Faculty

  • prof. Ms. Preeti Shukla
  • Ms. Riya Bangera

Course Level Learning Objectives

  • Developing appreciation for International literature and culture, reflect upon the ideas provided by the authors, and to articulate their own reflections on the topics.
  • As a part of Social Emotional Intelligence, students should be able to :

    • a. Develop Self-Awareness
    b. Manage their emotions
    c. Take Responsible Decisions
    d. Develop Social Awareness
    e. Build Relationship Skills

Assessment Structure

  • Continuous Assessment : 20 Marks
  • Internal Assessment Tests (IA) : 30 Marks
  • No End Term Examination.
